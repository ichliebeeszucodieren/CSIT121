Vegetable Rice

By my mom - a nice and satisfying meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 bell pepper
  • 2 carrots
  • 50 grams of beans
  • 1 potato
  • 1 onion (small single)
  • 1 cup of rice (Basmati)
  • 1⁄2 tsp of cumin seeds
  • 3 cloves
  • 2 bayleafs
  • pinch of cinnamon powder
  • 1⁄2 tsp of butter
  • 1⁄2 tsp of oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups of water

Directions/Steps

  1. Wash your rice twice and then soak the rice for 15 minutes
  2. Chop all the vegetables in the ingredients list to your liking
  3. In a heavy sauce pan (with a lid) add oil and butter
  4. Add cummin seeds, cloves, bay leaves, and cinammon powder to oil and butter
  5. Saute onions till it is caramelized
  6. Add rice and salt to taste
  7. Add in 2 cups of water
  8. Cover with lid and let the rice simmer for 10 minites
  9. After 10 minutes, add additional water as needed and make sure that the rice is throughly cooked
  10. Before serving, stir well!
  11. Enjoy it nice and hot with french fries or potato chips!
