About

Hello! Welcome to my final project website for CSIT 121! This website is a blog-like art portfolio website where you can see many pieces of my artwork and a commentary of my thought process behind it. I would like to not reveal much about my personal life, but I recently have gotten into art and I have found it to be calming and relaxing. For art enthusiants to people who just got bored, feel free to look around and get some inspirations!
